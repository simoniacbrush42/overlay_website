Foresight Overlay

A stats overlay for many gamemodes on hypixel

Features

Supports Multiple Gamemodes

Use this overlay to see the stats of the players in your hypixel games

    Gamemodes Supported: Bedwars, Skywars, Bridge Duels

Search

Search for the overall stats of any player on Hypixel

    Stats Tracked: Latest Login, Network Level, Bedwars, Skywars, Duels

Settings

Customize your settings for the best user experience

    More customization coming later

Helpful Commands

/who

⟶

adds all the players in your game to the overlay

/p list

⟶

adds all the players in your party to the overlay

/g list

⟶

adds all the players in your guild to the overlay

/w ![username]

⟶

adds an individual player to the overlay
